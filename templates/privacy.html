Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Jamaican Dream Interpreter provides cultural and educational dream interpretation tools. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected and used when you use this website.

Information You Provide

You may enter dream text, guided dream details, and an email address for access recovery or checkout. Dream journal entries are saved locally in your browser unless you choose to export or clear them.

Local Storage

This app may use browser localStorage to save your email, dream journal entries, and app preferences on your device. You can clear this information from inside the app or through your browser settings.

Interpretation Requests

When you tap Decode Dream, your dream text may be sent to the interpreter server so an interpretation can be generated.

Payments

Payments are handled by Stripe. We do not store your full payment card details on this website. Stripe may process payment information according to its own privacy and security policies.

Advertising

Free access may be supported by advertising. Advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve, measure, and improve ads. Paid access may reduce or remove ads inside the interpreter.

Cookies and Analytics

This site may use cookies, localStorage, analytics, or advertising tools to help operate the app, understand usage, improve performance, and support free access.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared with service providers needed to operate the app, such as hosting, payment processing, interpretation services, analytics, and advertising providers.

Medical, Legal, and Financial Disclaimer

Dream interpretations are cultural and educational only. They are not therapy, medical advice, legal advice, financial advice, or a substitute for professional help.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Jamaican True Stories through the main website: jamaicantruestories.com
